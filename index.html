Who I Am and What I Do

My name is Andrew. I’m a freelance web developer based in Toronto, Canada. I specialize in creating fully responsive websites that look great on any screen.

Why Do I Need A Responsive Site?


We live in a multi-device world. Gone are the days where you could assume your clients were reaching you on a computer. Today, you have visitors coming to your site from smartphones, tablets, and possibly even their watch.

To get the best results in this environment, you need a responsive website. Responsive websites look great on screens of all shapes and sizes. As a result, they’re better adapted to generate traffic, leads and sales in a multi-device world.

Put simply, a responsive website can help grow your business by tapping into conversion-friendly design features.

How Can You Help Me?


I can help you by building and publishing a beautiful responsive website that will serve as your business’ face to the world. That includes:

  1. Creating a unique design for your site. Includes color scheme, fonts, layouts and logo design.
  2. Building your site. Creating the moving pieces that make your site work on the web, whether that be with HTML and CSS, Wordpress, or any other CMS.
  3. Testing your site. Trying your website on a number of different screens to ensure that every single element--fonts, layouts, text and images--looks great on every device.
  4. Hosting your site. Finding the right host for your needs, registering your domain name and getting your site online.
